Hello! We are the **Center for Cryptography and Distributed Systems** at UBA.

## This blog

We write about what we learn along the way: cryptography, distributed systems architecture, useful tools, and our own projects.

## Technologies we use regularly

- **Rust** for systems and CLIs
- **Python** for scripting and data
- **Linux** as our main environment
- **Git** (of course)

## Contact

If you'd like to discuss any topic from the blog or collaborate on something, you can find us on GitHub or send us an email.
