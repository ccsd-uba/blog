## Purpose

The Center for Cryptography and Distributed Systems at UBA is dedicated to the study of technology and computer science areas related to cryptography, distributed systems, compilers, and system programming, generating new knowledge for academia, technology transfer, and the production of inputs that foster scientific and technological innovation.

## Functions

1. Conduct research on cryptography, concurrent programming, distributed systems, compilers, and related topics.
2. Disseminate results and findings through publications, events, and conferences.
3. Promote technology transfer and provide advisory services in its areas of expertise across all possible fields of application (private companies, public institutions, etc.).
4. Develop educational materials and carry out teaching activities in both public and private settings.
